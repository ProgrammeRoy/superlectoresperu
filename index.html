Hello! Dear friend we give you the most cordial welcome, knowing in advance that if you have reached this page, it is not by chance or pure coincidence because of the multiple experiences and testimonies of many children and adolescents that make up our reading family, we assure you that there is a very, very special purpose for which at this precise moment you find yourself reading these lines. Fun news! The ones I tell you about today in an exciting journey through this beautiful adventure that consists of starring in stories through the reading of beautiful and interesting books specialized in themes for both children and adolescents, and this is only the beginning. I tell you that as a child I did not like reading like many, until a teacher in elementary school made me experience through her special reading, that indescribable feeling of teleporting to the place where the story of the book took place. From that moment I couldn't wait to feel the sensation of lying in bed again after a long day, knowing that a good book was waiting for me, and that incomparable feeling that never left me is what we would love to be able to transmit to you. . The Little Readers are much more than a reading and writing club, because each program is designed to motivate, inspire and encourage critical learning that connects with that common sense that will progressively awaken in you, that avidity for more knowledge and deep wisdom... Of course , without losing the mystique that characterizes us, which is to read and learn while having fun! Best of all, you are freely invited to participate with us in this beautiful experience of sharing, co-creating spaces for integration and communion, so that, from these initiatives, you can develop various projects and artistic productions that will be generated over time. starting from specialized readings, aimed at promoting the application of values ​​and virtues that will make us more aware of our objective and that responsibility that we must contribute in and for our society. Therefore, you should know that we are always in time to take healthy smart actions that benefit the people and friends around us! Join us and be part of our beautiful group. It is that we want to hear your voice in these spaces where we read, write, sing, watch movies and share creative ideas with all the freedom to express our opinions, debate, and present our points of view on the topics we read. It is that each meeting is not only an invaluable cultural contribution, but also a celebration of and for reading and of course without forgetting the delicious Break, well, why should I tell you… Nutritionally Delicious! Let me tell you, that our meeting place is a dream, since it is located in a spacious country house, which in turn, has green areas where in the middle of nature you will breathe fresh air under the ceiling. You can also run freely and play under all control and security measures. We have diverse and multiple parlor games, logic blocks, legos and the funniest thing is that we develop dynamic group games, conversations and much more under the tutelage and instruction of professional psychologists... That's how we are when we think of you as the generation of today that will represent us in the very, very near future, and for this reason, we are committed to forming good citizens and, therefore, those professionals who have a high sense of ethics and morals that our nation needs. Thus, having adopted this noble purpose with each one of you, we strive to have everything you need to make your stay fun, pleasant and unforgettable, and thus, when you return home and insert yourself in the various areas of your day to day, you can put into practice everything you have learned. Part of the challenge that we have set for ourselves, and which, in turn, is part of our fundamental objective, is to reinforce continuity in each reading and idea that is reflected in direct reference to each of the processes of assimilation and understanding that ultimately leads to train readers who possess sensitivity and a critically authoritative opinion argument around the most important issues that concern our country as well as globally. What do you say?… Would you like to get or strengthen that magical habit of reading and making new friends? So don't miss out on this unique and great opportunity to be accompanied by your parents so that they too get involved both in your reading training and in your games and dynamics, well let me tell you, here there is also a very special space for them. . Well, in short, there is much more that I would like to tell you, but the best thing is that you visit us and we can get to know you and in this way, you can try this truly tremendous experience live and direct. We will wait for you!
